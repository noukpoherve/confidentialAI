Protection
Guardrail and data anonymization settings
Enable guardrail
Master switch. When off, no analysis is performed on any platform.
Auto-anonymize sensitive data
When on, the AI automatically redacts detected sensitive fragments instead of asking you to edit manually.

Example: "Contact me at john@company.com or +1 555 123 4567" becomes "Contact me at [EMAIL] or [PHONE]" before sending.

Content Moderation
Analyze prompts and AI responses for unsafe content
Enable content moderation
When enabled, prompts and generated responses are analyzed for policy risks (sensitive content, toxicity, and unsafe language). Disable this to keep only data anonymization and image moderation.

Example: a prompt containing insults or harassment is flagged before sending so you can edit it and avoid posting harmful content.

Image Moderation
Scan uploads for sensitive visual content
Enable image moderation
Every image you attach or paste is analyzed before upload. Detects sexual content, graphic violence, hate symbols, self-harm and harassment. Works on AI platforms and social networks.

Requires an OpenAI API key on the backend. Uses omni-moderation-latest. If no key is configured the extension fails open — uploads are not blocked.
